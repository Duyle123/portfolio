Video Proposal

What

As part of the overall experience, I'm going to be making a short documentary video. The goal of this documentary is to inform the audience about the lack of park space and access in the less affluent neighborhoods in NYC through interviews with people who've been affected, and also change makers who are actively fighting for their communities. There will be 2 main elements to focus on in this documentary - Sound and Image. Each will be produced independently but still complement each other in the final composition.

Image

The visual element of the documentary focuses primarily on showcasing the difference in park accessibility in multiple different neighborhoods. There won't be text that says which neighborhoods are the income of the households in the neighborhood. The audience should see and realize them themselves. The goal of the visual is not simply to “tell” the viewer that the poor areas are like this but rather to inform the viewer of the reality and whether they should do something about it.

Sound

The audio component will primarily consist of the interview audio between me and an agent of change. There will also be a mix of non-copyrighted background music and sound effects to enhance the viewing experience.

Interview questions details:

  1. Introduce topic
  2. Can you talk a little bit about your background and the organization that you're working for?
  3. What problems are you actively solving?
  4. How have these problems been addressed in recent years?
  5. How has your organization worked to solve this problem?
  6. How would one go about joining your organization or partake in your social activism?
  7. Do you have anything to say to the New Yorkers who are actively fighting for a climate change solution as a closing statement?

Why

The documentary serves as an informative, yet creative essay to confront the viewers on the topic of park accessibility in NYC. Thus discuss the environmental injustice that we are still fighting against everyday. Many people who live in NYC know about the amount of parks and greens that are available, yet are still not aware of the fact that these spaces are unequally distributed. The video hopefully can shed some light on this matter, and encourage people to act along with the agents of change.

How

In order to achieve my goals for this documentary, I'm going to devise a step-by-step plan for all three phases of production.

Phase 1: Pre-production:

  • Ideate & consult on concept
  • Storyboard sketch
  • Equipments needed for audio & image recording
  • Contact agents of change for interview opportunities

Phase 2: Production:

  • Interview agents of change (physical or online)
  • Shoot footage according to storyboard

Phase 3: Post-production:

  • Rough edit the footage in accordance with sounds
  • Draft 1 - polished edits
  • Draft 2 - polished colors and transitions
  • Finalized version

Equipments needed:

  • DSLR camera
  • Microphone - Audio Recorder
  • Laptop for editing
  • Software: Free-Version Davinci Resolve, Adobe Premiere Pro, Adobe Audition
