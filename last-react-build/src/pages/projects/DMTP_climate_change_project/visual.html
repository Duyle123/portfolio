The Documentary

The documentary is based on an interview I did with an NYU student who's been living in Queens for the past 4 years. He has experienced the park situation in New York City and share similar thoughts. In the video, I've combined a selection of clips from both the nicest, richest part of NYC as well as the poorest and most dangerous. I've also introduced two of the most active agent of change in the discussion of green spaces in the City - New Yorkers for Parks and the Fair Play Coalition. If you would like to learn more and show support, please click on the link here to go to their main page.
